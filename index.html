How to Play

  1. Select a game mode
  2. Choose one of the displayed choices
  3. Once you've selected a choice, it will say "Ready to Battle"
  4. Press "Fight" once both players are ready to battle
  5. Rock beats scissors and lizzard
  6. Paper beats rock and spock
  7. Scissors beats paper and lizzard
  8. Lizzard beats spock and paper
  9. Spock beats scissors and rock
