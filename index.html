How to Play

  1. Select Game mode
  2. Choose one of the displayed choices
  3. Press “Fight” button after selecting choice
  4. Its a best to 3, so whoever wins two rounds first wins
  5. Rock beats scissors and lizard
  6. Paper beats rock and Spock
  7. Scissors beats paper and lizard
  8. Lizard beats Spock and paper
  9. Spock beats scissors and rock
